The faculty at VJTI consists of highly qualified and experienced professionals. Many faculty members hold doctoral degrees and are actively involved in research. They provide mentorship and guidance to students, helping them excel in their academic pursuits. VJTI places a strong emphasis on research and development. The institute encourages students to participate in various research projects and workshops. Collaborative efforts with industries help bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical implementation. Student life at VJTI is vibrant and dynamic. The institute hosts various cultural and technical events throughout the year. These events provide students with opportunities to showcase their talents and learn from their peers. The annual technical festival, "Aarambh," attracts participants from various colleges. It features competitions, workshops, and guest lectures by industry leaders. This festival serves as a platform for students to network and gain exposure to real-world challenges.
